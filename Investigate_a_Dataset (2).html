Introduction¶

Dataset Description¶

Goal:To successfully analyse the dataset sample [No_show_appointments.csv] which I renamed to no_show.csv for easy accessibility,This dataset contains records of over 100,000 patients in Brazil with different disesases in which majority didn't show up for their fixed appointments on the scheduled day

The columns in this dataset I would be wirKing with are listed below and their significance;

1.PatientId: This identifies a patient.

2.AppointmentID: this signifies each patient's appointment number.

3.Gender: This signifies each gender i.e either male or female.

4.Scheduled Day:this indicates the appointment dates scheduled for each patient.

5.Appointment day:this shows the dates for each patient

6.Age: this column 'age' shows the different age of each patients.

7.Neighbourhood: thisshows the location.

8.Scholarship: indicates if the patient is a beneficiary of the brazilian welfare program(Bolsa Familia program).

9.Hipertension:a type of disease a patient has.

10.Diabetes: a type of disease apatient has.

11.Alcoholism: a type of disease a patient has.

12.Handcap: a type of disease a patient has.

13.SMS_received:this signifies messages sent to the patient.

14.No-show :"No" indicates if the patient showed up to their appointment and "Yes" if they didn't show up to their appointments
